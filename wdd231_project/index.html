Fresh, Local, Sustainable

Trellis Farm and Flowers is a family owned and operated urban farm located in Bluffdale, Utah. We specialize in growing high-quality, fresh produce and flowers using sustainable farming practices. Our mission is to provide local restraurants and consumers with the freshest, healthiest food possible while minimizing our environmental impact. We grow pesticide free greens, herbs and flowers using vertical farming techniques to maximize space and reduce water usage. We are committed to supporting our community and surrounding areas by providing fresh, healthy food options and promoting sustainable agriculture.

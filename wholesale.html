Wholesale Program

Reliable, consistent, and locally grown — our wholesale produce is available for restaurants, grocers, caterers, and distributors throughout Utah.

We provide weekly harvest availability lists, flexible minimum order quantities, and dedicated service for business customers. Partner with us to ensure your supply chain stays fresh and dependable.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Consistent Quality: All produce is harvested and packed the same day for superior freshness and shelf life.
  • Flexible Volume: Start small and scale as your demand grows.
  • On-Time Delivery: Coordinated delivery schedules and pickup options available.

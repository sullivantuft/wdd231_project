How Ordering Works

  1. Account setup: Submit business details and resale documentation.
  2. Catalog & availability: Receive weekly list with SKUs, grades, and packs.
  3. Place orders: Order by cutoff; allocations confirmed within 12 to 24 hours.
  4. Fulfillment: Delivery on route days or scheduled dock pickup.
  5. Payment: Net terms eligible after first order; otherwise due on receipt.
  • EDI/CSV ordering available
  • Case labeling with lot/harvest dates
  • Temperature-controlled handling where applicable

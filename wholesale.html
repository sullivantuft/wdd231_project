Wholesale Program

At Utah Farms & Flowers, our wholesale program is built on reliability, transparency, and partnership. We specialize in supplying restaurants, grocers, caterers, and local distributors across Utah with fresh, sustainably grown produce harvested at peak quality. Every delivery reflects the same hands-on care and commitment to consistency that defines our farm.

We know your business depends on dependable supply chains and uncompromising freshness. That’s why our wholesale operation runs on a structured, weekly harvest schedule and real-time availability updates. We offer flexible minimums, transparent pricing tiers, and logistics coordination that make planning and procurement effortless. Our dedicated team provides personal communication, fast response times, and adaptable solutions for seasonal menus, retail produce displays, and high-volume distribution alike.

Whether you’re a small restaurant seeking premium herbs and greens or a large distributor requiring consistent bulk orders, Utah Farms & Flowers ensures that freshness, flavor, and integrity never fluctuate. Partnering with us means working directly with growers who understand the importance of reliability, traceability, and long-term collaboration. Our goal isn’t just to fill orders—it’s to cultivate enduring partnerships that strengthen Utah’s local food network.

Why Partner With Us?

  • Consistent, Field-Leading Quality

    Harvested to order and packed the same day to protect texture, flavor, and shelf life. Uniform sizing and trim standards minimize prep time and waste.

    • Standardized specs per SKU (weight, trim, pack size)
    • Cold-chain aware packing and rapid handoff
    • Representative samples available for new buyers

  • Flexible Volume & Tiered Pricing

    Start small and scale without friction. Tiers reward predictable ordering while keeping entry minimums approachable.

    • Low starting minimums; case/each options
    • Tiers for 25–99, 100–299, and 300+ weekly units
    • Standing orders and forecast holds available

  • Reliable Logistics

    On-time delivery across the Wasatch Front on set route days, or convenient Bluffdale pickup. We coordinate windows so your team isn’t waiting.

    • Route days with 2-hour delivery windows
    • Text/email ETA notifications on dispatch
    • Temperature-appropriate packs and clear labels

  • Food Safety & Traceability

    Transparent inputs and lot-level tracking from seed to case. Pesticide-free growing with IPM and clean handling protocols.

    • Lot/harvest date on every case for FIFO rotation
    • Wash/handling SOPs and allergen separation
    • Rapid recall readiness (never used, always ready)

  • Menu & Merchandising Support

    Seasonal planning and substitution guidance to keep menus on track and produce sets full.

    • Weekly availability emails with early alerts
    • Spec sheets and storage tips per item
    • Chef/produce-manager cut sheets on request

  • Responsive Communication

    A single point of contact with fast response times during ordering and delivery windows.

    • Order by email or form; confirm within business hours
    • Day-of text line for urgent changes
    • Post-delivery issue resolution within 24 hours
